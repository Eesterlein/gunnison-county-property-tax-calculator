# Gunnison County Property Tax Calculator  
**Informational Overview**

## Overview

The **Gunnison County Property Tax Calculator** is an **educational, client-side tool** designed to help users understand how property taxes are calculated in Gunnison County, Colorado.

This tool is intended to provide transparency into the components of a property tax calculation and is **not an official tax calculator**.

**Live site:**  
https://eesterlein.github.io/gunnison-county-property-tax-calculator/

---

## Purpose of the Tool

The purpose of this calculator is to help property owners and members of the public:

- Understand how **actual value**, **assessment rates**, and **mill levies** interact
- See how different property types (residential, commercial, vacant land) are assessed
- Explore how changes in value or mill levies affect estimated taxes

This tool is provided for **educational and informational purposes only**.

---

## Data Sources

This calculator uses **publicly available Gunnison County data** that has been cleaned and converted to JSON format for client-side use.

- Data reflects a specific tax year and may **not represent other tax years**
- Tax district boundaries, mill levies, and property attributes may change annually
- The data shown may not reflect recent updates, corrections, or appeals

---

## How Property Taxes Are Calculated

Property taxes in Gunnison County are calculated using the following components:

1. **Actual Value**  
   - Determined by the Gunnison County Assessor’s Office based on market value

2. **Assessment Rates**  
   - Set by **Colorado state law**
   - Vary by property type (e.g., residential, commercial, vacant land)

3. **Mill Levies**  
   - Set by **local taxing authorities**, such as:
     - County
     - Municipalities
     - School districts
     - Special districts

The **Gunnison County Assessor’s Office does not set assessment rates or mill levies**.

---

## Estimates and Rounding

The estimates produced by this tool may differ from an official tax bill due to:

- Rounding methods used by official tax systems
- Exemptions (such as senior exemptions)
- Special district adjustments
- Statutory requirements or other administrative factors

Small differences can occur even when the same underlying values are used.

---

## Appeals and Exemptions

- **Taxes cannot be appealed**
- **Actual (market) value may be appealed** during the statutory appeal period
- Appeals apply only to the **assessed value**, not mill levies or tax rates
- Exemptions and adjustments are **separate from the appeal process**

For official appeal procedures and deadlines, property owners should consult:
https://www.gunnisoncounty.org/953/Appeals

---

## Limitations

This calculator:

- Does not account for all exemptions, abatements, or special circumstances
- Does not replace official Notices of Valuation or tax statements
- Should not be used for financial planning or legal determinations

**Official county records and tax statements control all tax calculations.**
